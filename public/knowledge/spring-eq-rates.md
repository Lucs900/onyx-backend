**Margin over Prime**

### Product Terms
- 3-Year Initial Draw Period
- 10-Year Interest Only Payment Period during draw
- Then converts to fully amortizing repayment:
  - Option 1: 20-Year fully amortizing repayment period (Total term ~30 years)
  - Option 2: 17-Year fully amortizing repayment period (Total term ~27 years)
  - Option 3: 27-Year fully amortizing repayment period (Total term ~30 years)

### Line Amount / Initial Draw Rules
- Minimum Line Amount: Greater of $25,000 or 75% of the total line amount
- Maximum Line Amount: $500,000
- Lines ≥ $400,000 require minimum 740 FICO
- Additional draws are prohibited during the first 90 days following closing

### Rate Buy-Up / Buy-Down
- For every 5 bps change in rate, the price changes by 12.5 bps (2.5x multiple)
- Maximum price = 102

### Important Notes
- All rates are wholesale rates
- 30 & 45 day rate locks are reflected in the pricing above
- Buydown option is **not available** in: CO, DC, IN, MD, MI, MS, NC, VT, VA, WA
- Texas has special admin fee rules ($395 if < $70k, $695 if ≥ $70k)
- Only 4 items typically needed for initial underwriting: Income docs, Homeowner’s insurance, First mortgage statement, Photo ID
