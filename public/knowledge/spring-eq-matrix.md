**Release Date:** 5/4/2026  
**Focus:** HELOC & HELOAN Guidelines (FICO, CLTV, Terms, Requirements)

## Product Terms – HELOC & HELOAN

**HELOC (Variable Rate)**
- 3-Year Draw Period + 10-Year Interest Only + 20-Year fully amortizing repayment (30 Year Term)
- 3-Year Draw Period + 10-Year Interest Only + 17-Year fully amortizing repayment (27 Year Term)
- 3-Year Draw Period + 10-Year Interest Only + 27-Year fully amortizing repayment (30 Year Term)

**HELOAN (Fixed Rate)**
- 10, 15, 20, or 30 Year fully amortizing terms available
- 3-Year Draw + 10-Year Interest Only + 20-Year amortizing (some products)
- 3-Year Draw + 3-Year Interest Only + 27-Year amortizing (some products)

## Loan or Line Amounts

- **Minimum Line/Loan Amount**: $25,000
- **Maximum Line Amount (HELOC)**: $500,000
- **Maximum Loan Amount (HELOAN)**: $500,000
- Lines or loans ≥ $400,000 require minimum 740 FICO

## HELOC Additional Draws
- Prohibited during the first 90 days after closing
- All additional draws must be completed via ACH
- Minimum additional draw: $1,000
- Borrower must submit one draw request per calendar month

## Debt to Income (DTI)
- Max 50% DTI
- Max 45% DTI with a minimum 740 FICO **or** $3,500 monthly residual income
- DTI calculated using the initial draw payment for HELOCs

## Maximum Total Financing
- Primary Residence & Second Home: Max 90% CLTV (combined 1st + 2nd)
- Investment Properties: Max 80% CLTV
- Owner-Occupied: Limited to $3,000,000 total (1st + 2nd combined)
- Investment: Limited to $2,000,000 total

## Housing Payment History
- No mortgage lates in last 12 months
- No mortgage lates in last 24 months on the subject property
- No bankruptcy, foreclosure, or short sale in last 48 months (some exceptions apply)

## Credit Score Requirements
- Credit score used is the lowest Experian score (version 2) of all borrowers
- Non-traditional credit is **not** permitted
- Must have at least one credit score available

## Tradeline Requirements
Must show **one** of the following:
- Minimum 3 tradelines (at least one open with activity in last 6 months and reporting for 12+ months)
- OR a current mortgage paid as agreed for the past 36 months

## Self-Employed Income
**Self-Employed ≥ 5 years**
- Most recent year personal + business tax returns (all pages & schedules)
- Most recent year 1040 tax transcripts

**Self-Employed < 5 years**
- Most recent 2 years personal + business tax returns
- Most recent 2 years 1040 tax transcripts

## Additional Requirements
- **Value Seasoning**: Use lower of purchase price or current appraised value (0–6 months)
- **Ownership Seasoning**: Primary & Second Home – 6 months minimum ownership
- **Eligible Properties**: Single family, PUDs, condos, 1-4 unit properties (borrower occupies one unit)
