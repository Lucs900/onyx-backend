# Spring EQ Wholesale Fees Breakdown
**Version:** 4.20.26 v.2

## Administration Fees

| Product          | Administration Fee                  | Notes |
|------------------|-------------------------------------|-------|
| FIXLINE (Fixed HELOC) | $999 (or $500 if line < $50,000)   | Texas: $395 if < $70k, $695 if ≥ $70k |
| HELOC (Adjustable)    | $999 (or $500 if line < $50,000)   | — |
| HELOAN (Fixed)        | $799 (or $500 if loan < $50,000)   | Texas: $395 if < $70k, $695 if ≥ $70k |

**Annual Maintenance Fee** (applies to both FIXLINE and HELOC)  
- $99 per year (charged on anniversary date)

**Important Notes on Fees**
- Fees marked with ** are **not** charged on Piggyback transactions (purchase or refinance).
- Spring EQ pays for the appraisal review on all Piggyback loans (no cost to borrower).
- A full ALTA title policy is required on Spring EQ’s loan.
- All fees are estimates. Final fees are disclosed within 3 days of application.
- Fees are calculated based on the information provided at registration.
